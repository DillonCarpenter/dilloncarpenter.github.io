    Chess Trainer

    November 27, 2025

    Goals:#

    Create a post game analysis tool for chess players.

    What is the problem to solve?#

    One of the best ways for chess players to improve is to analyze and review their games. This is typically done with the help of a strong chess engine like Stockfish. The problem? Current chess engines provide evaluations but not explanations.

    Why this problem is important to solve?#

    This makes it difficult for learners and even advanced players to understand the underlying ideas behind moves.

    How do I solve this problem?#

    My project is to build a tool that generates human-like explanations of engine suggestions, focusing on tactical and strategic motifs by parsing through Stockfish’s output.
